fbref visualised

All data from fbref.com and transfermarkt.com. Data begins from the 17/18 season. fbref data updated 30/10/22 07:42 GMT. transfermarkt values updated 23/11/22 13:45 GMT. Data is grouped by categories and can be displayed on either axis. Hover over the markers for more information about a player. To query a sub-set of players interact with the legend and the widgets on the left. Ratios (such as goals per shot), percentages and minutes are always shown as totals, all other values can be shown as totals or per minute. To highlight a player click on their marker or enter their name into the search box. Percentiles and distribution plots are calculated for players with over 1350 minutes played and are always per minute, except for ratios and minutes. Name labels can be added or removed using the bottom selection widget.
